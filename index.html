How to Pick Fruit Off Palm Trees Properly

On Survivor, you not given that much food, the only kinds of food that you're going to get on Survivor are reward foods that you must win, rice by coming to a deal with Jeff Probst at a merge immunity challenge, the fish in the ocean, and the fruit on nearby palm trees from your camp.

On the palm trees at your camp, there are papayas,coconuts, mangoes, bananas, and pineapples, and this webpage will show you how to pick those kinds of fruits off of the palm trees properly so that way, you can fill your stomach with healthy snacks while you're at your tribe camp.
How to Pick Coconuts to Get Coconut Water
  1. Gently tip the back of the machete or rowboat oar against the top of the coconut to remove the cap where the stem was attached.
  2. Tap the back of the machete or oar against the husk to create a narrow grove.
  3. Hold the coconut vertically with the chopped of cap facing up and use the hook of the machete or oar to stab into the husk near the top, about an inch from the groove you've created.
  4. Twist the machete or oar away from the groove to split off the coconut husk.
  5. Lightly tap the centerline of the coconut with the back of the machete or oar to crack open the coconut and get the water inside of it.
How to Pick the Other Fruits Off of the Palm Trees
  • For papayas and mangoes, use the same technique you use to chop off coconuts.
  • For bananas, make diagonal cuts around the branch holding them up.
  • For pineapples, carefully cut them, leaving 2-3 inches of the stem that held them up.
